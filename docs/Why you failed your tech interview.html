Introduction

Cramming LeetCode will never cover up a lack of fundamentals. Your interviewers will not be fooled. Before you start bashing away at a new problem, ask yourself if you have the right tool for the job; by tool I mean fundamentals.

Fundamentals

  • Recursion
    • Draw a diagram
  • Algorithmic Analysis
    • Know the runtimes for operations on basic data structures
    • Understand the mergesort analysis
    • Understand the most common amortized analyses

Environment

  • Practice on a whiteboard, or coder pad.
  • Have a good phone screen setup: headphones, microphone, etc.
