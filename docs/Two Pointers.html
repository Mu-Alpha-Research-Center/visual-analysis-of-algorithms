Two Pointers

It's similar to the sliding window but now your pointers are moving around based on certain rules. A good example is two sum on a sorted array.

  • Problems with sorted arrays where you need to find a set of elements that fulfil certain critera. For, example, two sum on a sorted array.
  • The set of elements is a pair, triplet or even a subarray.

Variations

  • Low & high pointer: Two sum sorted. Pointers are converging towards to the center. This can also work for squaring a sorted array.
  • Fan out: Squaring sorted array. Pointers are moving out to the edges or fanning out to the ends of the array.
  • Sliding window is a variation of 2 pointers. Pointers are moving either in lock stop, or dynamically from left to right across the structure.
  • Slow pointer fast pointer. One is moving slow and the other is moving fast.

Examples

  • Two sum sorted array: https://leetcode.com/problems/two-sum-ii-input-array-is-sorted
